At Whitewater Adventures we don't take the word "adventure" lightly. Every adventure is designed to be a thourough cure for boredom and monotony.

History

Since as long as humans have been around we have worked together in groups to do difficult things and have lived full of thrill and excitement. Our ancestors overcame massive obsticles to spread out accross the entire world White water rafting is a way to put back some of the thrill and teamwork back into modern life.

Since as long as our company has been around, we have sought to bring that thrill to the lives of people who are looking for a little excitement. Since 1991, we have guided people through the rush of White water rafting, and seen the beginning of many lifelong friendships as a result.
