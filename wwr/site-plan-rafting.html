White Water Rafting Site Plan

Zac Jensen

Overview

Purpose

The main purpose of the website is to attract more customers to the white water rafting service and bring more awareness to the company by ranking in the relevant search engine results for the region.

Audience

The target audience will generally be fit, energetic, thrill-seeking young adults. they are likely to be above average in extraversion and openess. They are also likely to be somewhere in the range of middle class with a fair amount of disposable income. The white water rafting servive will satisfy a customer looking for a thrill in the great outdoors. The target audience will also be more likely to be fairly tech savvy and competent with devices, and will be most likely to access the website on a mobile device like a phone.
