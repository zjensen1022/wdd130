Avaliable Trips

Half Day Getaway

Dive into the world of whitewater rafting with a half day of adventure. Whether this is your first time rafting or you just need a short fix this short adventure is a great way to have a short adventure.

Full Day of Rapids

If half a day doesn't do it for you, you can sign up for a full day of adventure that will keep you on your toes. Fair warning though, you may find yourself wanting even more rafting after a full day of fun.

Full Weekend Trip

If a full day doesn't do it for you, take the full weekend trip! Experience two full days in nature getting exercise and meeting new people. If you haven't been white water rafting before, one of the other trips may be the best option.
